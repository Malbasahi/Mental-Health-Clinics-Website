Geting Help

If you are concerned that you are developing a mental health problem you should seek the advice and support of your GP as a matter of priority. You can contact the appropriate agencies in case you are in a situation and need emergency support and unavailable to see a GP.

The Mind Garden is a site specialising in research and policy development, with a focus on preventing mental health problems. So we are not able to advise people directly on their personal circumstances.

If you would like advice about looking after your mental health during the Coronavirus outbreak visit this page.
