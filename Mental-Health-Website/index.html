Mental health

Mental health is an essential aspect of general well-being and health. Our mental, psychological and social well-being requires mental wellbeing. It influences our thinking, feeling and acting.
It also encourages us to decide how we deal with tension, connect with others, and choose healthily.
In any life cycle, from infancy to adolescence to adulthood, mental wellbeing is essential.
